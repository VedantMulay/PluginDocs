## Commands and Permissions

| Command                   	| Permission                   	|
|---------------------------	|------------------------------	|
|`/plogger getlogs <username>` 	| `playerlogger.command.getlogs` 	|
|`/plogger firstjoinlocation <username>` | `playerlogger.command.firstjoinlocation`|
|`/plogger lastlogoutlocation <username>` | `playerlogger.command.lastlogoutlocation`|
|`/plogger getchatlogs <username> [newest \| oldest] <limit>` | `playerlogger.command.getchatlogs`|
|`/plogger getcommandlogs <username> [newest \| oldest] <limit>` | `playerlogger.command.getcommandlogs`|
|`/plogger reload`|`playerlogger.command.reload`|
